Our Story

Rooted in Purpose,
Growing in Grace

The Baobab at Rhandzu began as a seed of intention — a mother and daughter's dream to build something meaningful. Starting with The Firm Health & Slimming Franchise in 2021, we discovered that people needed more than physical transformation. They needed healing of the whole self.

In October 2024, that vision blossomed into a full-service luxury spa, inspired by the strength and longevity of the baobab tree — a symbol of resilience, community, and deep African roots. Every treatment we offer carries this spirit.
